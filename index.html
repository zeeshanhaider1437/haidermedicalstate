Haider medical state includes an institute and a medical college alongside an AI research centre. It contains modern facilities for learning medicine and treatment of patients. The institute trains students in various medical fields after matriculation. It has highly qualified staff accompanied with lecture halls havig visual aids and labs for practical learning. The college offers various courses: FSC, dispenser, lab technician, OT technician, ECG and X-ray technician. The hospital has various departments like emergency, outdoor, indoor wards, and operation theatres. Consultation by different practitioners is provided daily. AI education facilities are provided to students through a computer lab.

Three campuses are located in different areas of the city.
